I am a **Senior Researcher** at **StepFun**, where I work with Dr. [Gang Yu](https://www.skicyyu.org/). My current research centers on **edge-side multimodal foundation models** — unifying conversational, multimodal-understanding, and agentic capabilities in a single model. I am also broadly interested in **AIGC image foundation models**, with a particular focus on **image editing** and **controllable generation**.

Earlier in my career, I spent several years on **3D vision and computer graphics**. Before StepFun, I held research positions at **Tencent** and **SenseTime Research**. I graduated from the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology**, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Lu Fang](http://www.luvision.net/) and Prof. [Yebin Liu](http://www.liuyebin.com/).
