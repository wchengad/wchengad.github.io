I am a **Senior Researcher** at **StepFun**, where I work with Dr. [Gang Yu](https://www.skicyyu.org/). My current research focuses on **edge AI** and **on-device multimodal foundation models**, bringing conversational, multimodal-understanding, and agentic capabilities into compact models for resource-constrained devices. I am also broadly interested in **generative image foundation models**, especially **image editing** and **controllable generation**.

Earlier in my career, I worked on **3D vision and computer graphics**. Before joining StepFun, I held research positions at **Tencent** and **SenseTime Research**. I graduated from the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology**, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Lu Fang](http://www.luvision.net/) and Prof. [Yebin Liu](http://www.liuyebin.com/).
